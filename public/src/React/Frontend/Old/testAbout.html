About Porsche

Porsche is a German luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche. The company is known for producing high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. Porsche has a rich history in motorsports and has achieved numerous victories in prestigious races. The brand's mission is to create exceptional driving experiences and innovative automotive solutions. With a commitment to quality and craftsmanship, Porsche continues to be a symbol of automotive excellence.

Porsche was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart, Germany. The iconic Porsche 911 was introduced in 1963 and remains one of the most recognizable sports cars. The company has a strong presence in motorsports, with numerous victories at events like Le Mans. In 2002, Porsche introduced its first SUV, the Porsche Cayenne, expanding its product lineup. The electric Porsche Taycan, introduced in 2019, showcases the brand's commitment to sustainability.

1931: Foundation of Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche GmbH by Ferdinand Porsche. 1963: Introduction of the Porsche 911, an iconic and enduring model. 1972: The Porsche 911 Turbo was the first production sports car with an exhaust turbocharger. 2003: Launch of the Porsche Cayenne, marking the brand's entry into the SUV market. 2019: Unveiling of the Porsche Taycan, the first all-electric sports car from Porsche.
